Mahabaleshwar is a tourist spot in central India conveniently located near the financial capital of India, Mumbai. It is a favorite weekend getaway destination for city dwellers for rejuvenating themselves away from the hustle bustle of the city and tourist across all over the country. The breathtaking landscapes, numerous lakes and rivers and strawberry farms attracts thousands of tourists to enjoy this small yet impeccably maintained wonder of the nature. Mahabaleshwar is famous for its rich heritage, being a place pilgrimage for Hindus, it has the famous Krishna river originating here and being developed as a hill station under British colonial rule, It has still has that summer capital vibe that enchants every tourist visiting this beautiful and exotic city.
